What are the payment options available

We accept payments via bank transfer, credit/debit card, and mobile money platforms such as M-Pesa. Our website provides a secure checkout process for your convenience.

Do you offer customization options for furniture pieces

Yes, we specialize in custom-made furniture. You can choose from a variety of wood types, finishes, dimensions, and designs to create a piece that perfectly suits your needs and preferences.

Can you create furniture pieces based on specific designs or inspirations

Absolutely! We love bringing our customers' unique visions to life. Whether you have a specific design in mind or need assistance in conceptualizing your ideas, our skilled craftsmen are here to turn your vision into reality.

How long does it take to receive a custom-made furniture order

The production time for custom orders varies depending on the complexity of the design and our current workload. On average, it takes between 2 to 8 weeks from the time of order placement to delivery.

What types of wood do you use for your furniture

We primarily use locally sourced and imported hardwoods such as mahogany and mvule for our furniture pieces. These woods are known for their durability and natural beauty.We also use use Mango which represents our environmental commitment while creating beautiful furniture pieces.

Are your materials sustainably sourced

We are committed to sustainability and ethical practices. Our wood is sourced from responsibly managed forests and certified suppliers to ensure minimal environmental impact.

What maintenance is required for solid wood furniture

Solid wood furniture requires minimal maintenance to preserve its beauty and longevity. Regular dusting and occasional polishing with a high-quality wood conditioner are recommended. Avoid placing furniture in direct sunlight or near heat sources to prevent warping or discoloration.

Do you offer warranty or guarantees on your products

Yes, we stand behind the quality of our products. We offer a one-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Please refer to our warranty policy for more details.

What should I do if my furniture arrives damaged

Notify us immediately. Our team will assess and repair the issue promptly. Refunds or replacements are provided if necessary.

Do you deliver outside Nairobi

Yes, we offer nationwide delivery across Kenya. Additional shipping charges may apply for deliveries outside Nairobi. Please contact us for more information.
