Vector Woodcrafts FAQs

How can I place an order

Once you've chosen your furniture, add them to your shopping cart. Each item has its price listed. The prices of items are not inclusive of VAT. Once you checkout your selected products, and have completed the payment, your order will be sent to us.

What are the payment options available

We accept cash, M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cheques. If paying by cheque, delivery will occur after clearance.

Where do you deliver

We offer free delivery service within Nairobi.

Is there a catalogue available

You can download our catalogue from the DOWNLOADS section of our website.

How can I check the availability of a specific item

Contact us via phone or email, and we'll gladly update you on its availability. Please note, availability isn't guaranteed until payment.

What happens during delivery

You'll be contacted in advance to arrange delivery. Our team will position the furniture as instructed, remove packaging, and ensure clear access points. Someone must be present to receive and sign for the furniture.

What if I'm not available during delivery

As deliveries require a signature, if you're unavailable, the furniture will be returned to the distribution center. A further delivery date will be arranged with an additional charge.

Can I choose a specific delivery day or time

Due to limited storage, we aim to deliver swiftly and appreciate your flexibility.

Do you offer furniture disposal services

Unfortunately, we don't provide this service. Prompt delivery helps us offer the best value.

What if my furniture doesn't fit

Please ensure the furniture fits your space before ordering. For office spaces, our design team provides free measurements and advice.

Do you deliver internationally

No, we don't offer international delivery. Customers can arrange their own shipping.

What if I'm unsatisfied with my delivered furniture

Unfortunately, we can't accept returns as items are specially chosen and delivered as requested.

What should I do if my furniture arrives damaged

Notify us immediately. Our team will assess and repair the issue promptly. Refunds or replacements are provided if necessary.

How can I address a complaint with my order

Please contact us via email or phone with any concerns. We're dedicated to resolving issues promptly.
